Our Academic Programs

CZS @ LASU Farm Land

Covenant of Zion Schools embarked on an educational excursion to Lagos State University's Farm Land, fostering a rich learning experience for our students. This immersive journey allowed them to explore and understand agricultural practices firsthand. We believe in providing holistic education, and this excursion aimed to cultivate a deeper appreciation for the vital connection between theory and practical knowledge. It was an enriching experience that aligns with our commitment to nurturing well-rounded and informed individuals at Covenant of Zion Schools.

CZS @ Spelldemic 2023

Covenant of Zion Schools is thrilled to be a part of Spelldemic 2023, an event that celebrates knowledge and learning. As an institution committed to academic excellence, we eagerly anticipate engaging with fellow educators, sharing insights, and contributing to the enriching atmosphere of Spelldemic 2023. Join us as we embrace the spirit of education and innovation at Covenant of Zion Schools' presence in this prestigious gathering.

CZS on Excursion trip

Covenant of Zion Schools' excursion trip became more than just a journey; it became a memorable chapter in the students' educational journey. CZS's vision for a well-rounded educational experience was vividly realized as students not only gained valuable insights from the excursion but also forged lasting bonds, making education an adventure to be cherished at CZS.
