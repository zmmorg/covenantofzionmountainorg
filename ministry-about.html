PASTOR/DR. MICHAEL BABAJIDE ORESEGUN A.K.A TIOBAOGO.

APOSTLE/DR. MICHAEL BABAJIDE ORESEGUN A.K.A TIOBAOGO is the General Pastor of Zion Mountain Ministry for all Nations. He is also the current Leader/Chairman of Christian Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria (C.P.F.N) and Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria (P.F.N) Bloc of Christian Association of Nigeria (C.A.N), Ejigbo Local Council Development Area (L.C.D.A), Lagos, Nigeria.
An Apostle and a Prophet by calling, a Pastor per excellence, Priest of the MOST HIGH GOD, a commissioned Evangelist, and a seasoned Teacher of the WORD for the world. Well Groomed and Supernaturally Endowed with Pentecostal Power and Authority by CHRIST JESUS, through HIS HOLY SPIRIT to the Glory of GOD MOST HIGH for End time Global Harvest.
A true man of GOD, with years of experience in the ministry, cool-headed, loving, humble, easy going, outstanding entrepreneur, a godly genius, great disciplier, obedient to heavenly vision, charismatic, vibrantly full of fire with Heavenly backing according to the working effect of the GOD of Prophet Ezekiel, made evidence in us through CHRIST JESUS the KING of KINGS.
A Zionite, an alien on earth, a citizen of heaven, born 26th of December to the family of Chief Stephen Adesanya and Mrs Florence Ekundayo Oresegun, from Agbowa-Ikosi, Epe, Lagos, Nigeria. Happily married to Evangelist (Mrs.) Esther Olubunmi Oresegun TIOBAOGO the General Pastor (Mrs.) at Zion Mountain Ministry For All Nations and Proprietress at Covenant of Zion Schools. A graduate of Mass Communication and a seasoned post graduate Educator, blessed with children through the infinite mercy of JESUS CHRIST to the GLORY of MOST HIGH GOD of ZION.

Once a football player invited for screening at the golden eaglet camp, Onikan Stadium 1992/1993 squared. He is the Founder and President/Manager of United-Warriors Football Clubs (a.k.a Jendor Babes) established in 1987. After quitting active football, established:
•JIDE NA GENIUS for Mobile Phones Repairs, Training and Sales Centres in 2003.
•OCTAGON INTERNATIONAL AGENCY in 2008 for small scale real estate agency, traveling consultancy and sports management.
•OCZEG GLOBAL CONTRACTORS LTD in 2010 for big scale real and chattels property agencies, traveling consultancy and sports management.
Till date he is the MD/CEO of the companies, and also stand as consultant for some other companies to the glory of the GOD of Heaven and earth.

EXAMINING SOME LEADERSHIP IMPACT OF GOD through Pastor ‘Jide Oresegun, viewing his role as Youth President of Estate Baptist Church, Oke-Afa, Isolo, Lagos.

•On the Platform as Youth President (2001-2004) together with the yearly Youth week officials, Youth Executive, Youth PPC, the Youth body, and with the prayer support of the Pastor in charge (Revd. M. O. LAWAL), pioneered the:
- First ever December 26th ‘JESUS CARNIVAL’ now been embraced by many churches Nation-Wide (An interdenominational musical concert festival involving many churches, Gospel Bands, with Local and International Gospel Artist) thereby countering the world worldly carnival which comes up same date. - Under him also came the African Praise Concert every Youth Week, Quarterly interdenominational Praise Night for all, Singles Night which comes up in June. And the Youth Week Press Release that humorously gives both the summary of past and future socio-spiritual activities of the church. - Well loved, his era brought about a reign of love, unity and Peace within the Youth Organisation and the church at large by extension. - With virtually no clear-cut structure on ground, he re-organised the youth organisation on a foundation of LOVE, UNITY and FRIENDLINESS, leading by example. - The reorganisation of Youth Organisation by him birth over 12 departments and over 20 units under is direct supervision through Divine Leading. - The Youth was able on it own able to raise over One Hundred Thousand Naira (#100,000:00) every June for her Youth week, and above that for her December programme. Making her less dependant on the church financially. - Lunched the first ever Magazine for both the youth and the church and Youth Constitution. - Staged the first ever Prestigious Youth Week ‘Award Day’ in June to reward past youth executives, and December ‘Award Night’ to reward outstanding youths. - Dinner Night and Love Feast as a forum for togetherness, to bring new youths into the body, win souls and populate the church. - Established ‘Night For Two’ (couples night) to educate and celebrate about to wed youths. And as forum to pray for them and promote purity, godly courtship, and holy-wedlock amidst youths. - Youth Evangelism which brought in new youths into the church.
